Butterfly Knife | Black Laminate

Knife • Battle Scarred • Float: 0.9708 (Rank #132 Highest) • Pattern 846

After a long while, i finally decided to pick up a Butterfly Knife, and it was worth it.

Back when i was younger, long before i even played the game (i've been into Counter-Strike since 2016) i always loved the butterfly knife. The smooth animations, rare pullout - this knife has everything you want.

I ended up picking the black laminate because it goes with everything in my loadout. Because it's... well... black-. I am very glad i finally get to own one of these knives because as bad as it sounds owning one of these was kind of a childhood dream i thought i wasn't going to achieve in my lifetime.
