Driver Gloves | Queen Jaguar

Gloves • Minimal Wear • Float: 0.1419 • Pattern 994 (Tier 1)

The story of me and these gloves go way back. I've owned 4 pairs of these gloves and this is the current pair that i own. Let me tell you about my story with these gloves and why they are my favorite to this day, even though they are not that pricey.

These gloves mean a lot to me. I was first introduced to these gloves in early 2024, when i bought a battle scarred pair as my 2nd pair of gloves ever. I loved them, they radiated swag and truly felt personal. However, i then ended up selling them to pick up a Talon. I missed them ever since

I was re-introduced to these gloves by Ryder, a person i met on a Discord server about mapping. Just like me, he was really into skins. But his favorite pair of gloves were the Queen Jaguars, or the 'Jags'. He would always brag about how these gloves were better than all other gloves in the game and how everyone should own a pair. He literally has a steam group, guide, alternate account, cat. Everything in his life is themed after these gloves bassically. And he made me buy a Field Tested pair not too long after

After owning 2 Field Tested pairs of these, i finally decided to upgrade to Minimal Wear. And it's really worth it because the knuckles look a lot better. Make sure to check out Ryder's guide on what the best patterns of Jags are. These gloves will always have a place in my heart and i think i will always come back to them. The way that i get complimented for these more then anything, even though they are on the lower end of price. I just love them, everyone should own a pair.
