Probably one of my favorite AK's in my loadout, the First Class is a skin that i go way back with...

Also, the name tag. I called it "La Ventura", naming it after the first Counter-Strike 2 map i made. Due to this being my first expensive AK-47 i bought. I put 4 MIBR Holos on it due to them not only having the green on the sticker but also the brown that is on the grip on the rear of the gun.

I have also sold and loaned out this skin 2 times now. I loaned it out once to good friend Gochujang (who you should check out on twitter, he makes great renders). And, i also sold this skin to my good friend Chris in 2025 summer. I bought it back in December.

Due to this skin not wearing a lot (and due to me being broke) i bought it in Field Tested condition. This skin is probably never leaving me or my friend's inventory. I will hold this skin dearly until i can't. And when i can't, i will pass it onto a friend.
